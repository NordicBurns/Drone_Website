Ardiuno Coding Software

Our drone was 3d printed and wired together by hand. We used an ESP32, Arduino Board, ESC, and a breadboard to make this drone come to life!

The lessons we learned while making this drone were
-Team Coordination
-Coding Arduino Software
-How to Solder
-How to Wire a Drone
-Better Communication Skills
-Wire Safety
-How to Build a Drone from Scratch
